Sri Mariyamman Temple

Goddess Mariamman temple also called as Mari ellaiyamman temple. The Main deity of the temple goddess mariamman along with renuga devi (the deity of another form of goddess present under the foot of mariamman. Only head portion of renuga devi is worshipped). The goddess is seated facing the east. There are two vinayagars present in the temple and also there is 5 headed snake statue outside,present straighly facing the goddess. Devotees perform rituals mainly in the month of July and August (Aadi poojai). The goddess is mainly worshipped for prevention and curing Chicken pox.
